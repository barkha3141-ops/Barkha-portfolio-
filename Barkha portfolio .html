about

Hello [hola]

I’m Barkha from Hyderabad currently in Manipal pursuing my bachelor’s in Graphic Design and Digital Marketing.

Somewhere between late night overthinking playlists that hit too hard and sketching ideas that didn’t exist an hour ago I found my thing. Design isn’t just what I do it’s how I process the world. Every color I pick every curve I refine every layout I obsess over it all says something I couldn’t explain out loud.

I’m drawn to contrast. Clean but expressive. Structured but a little rebellious. I like when things make sense but I love when they make you feel something first.

I’ve spent time getting lost questioning restarting unlearning and that’s exactly what shaped my perspective. I don’t create perfect work I create honest work. Work that evolves just like I do.

Right now I’m exploring experimenting and building. Learning the rules breaking them and then rewriting them in my own way.

This isn’t just a portfolio.
It’s a glimpse into how I see think and feel through design.
